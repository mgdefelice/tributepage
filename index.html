Time Line

  • 1967 - Born July 15th, 1967 in New York City. Grandfather was a world renowned surgeon, and father, a painter, filmmaker and animator who was best known for his work on Sesame street.
  • 1985 - Graduated from Sleepy Hollow Highschool in Westchester County, NY.
  • 1985 - Appeared in the music video You're only human by Billy Joel.
  • 1990 - In the summer Adam headed west to San Francisco and became apart of the thriving theater sceen, working in set design, prop maker, art director scenic painter, rigger,master carpenter and producer
  • 1998 - Adam left the theater scene to work in R&D for toy company Zoob
  • 2000 - Began working with George Lucas company Industrial Lights, where he worked on films such as Star Wars, Episodes 1 & 2, Galaxy Quest, Terminator 3, Matrix Reloaded, The Mummy and many more. Some with his future co host Jamie Hyneman.
  • 2000 - Taught five semesters at the Academy of Arts College in San Francisco, teaching advanced model making and Problem Solving.
  • 2002 - Got a call from Jamie Hyneman to co host a Tv show in which they test popular myths
  • 2004 - Adam married his wife Julia Ward
  • 2003 - In January Mythbusters first aired, and three weeks later it was picked up by the discovery chanel.
  • 2012 - Began co-hosting a podcast Still Untitled: the Adam Savage Project. The Podcast was a formal converstation between Will Smith, Norman Chan, and Simone Giertz on topics ranging from science and movies to making and work ethics.
  • 2012 - Adam takes over and relaunches the website tested.com
  • 2016 - In March of 2016 the final Episode of Mythbusters Airs. The show aired a total of
  • 2017 - Named Humanist of the year by the American Humanist Association.
  • 2017 - Played a cameo role on the prequel movie to Blade Runner 2049.
  • 2018 - The Discovery Chanel announced the Savage will host and produce a new series called MythBusters Jr, a series focused on a group of young scientist tasked with completing varios experiments of challenges through the use of STEAM Skills.
